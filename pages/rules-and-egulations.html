Rules & Regulations

To ensure a safe, clean, and motivating environment for all our members, please review and follow these guidelines at all times while on the premises.

1. General Conduct

  • Members must always behave respectfully toward staff and fellow members.
  • Discrimination, harassment, or inappropriate language is strictly prohibited.
  • Proper athletic attire and footwear are required at all times.

2. Membership

  • Memberships are non-transferable and non-refundable unless stated otherwise.
  • All members must check in at the front desk upon arrival.
  • Any changes in contact information should be reported to the front desk immediately.

3. Facility Usage

  • Wipe down equipment after use with the provided sanitizers.
  • Return all weights and equipment to their designated places.
  • Use lockers for storing personal belongings. FitZone is not responsible for lost or stolen items.
  • Time limits may be imposed on cardio equipment during peak hours (30 minutes max).

4. Group Classes

  • Please arrive at least 5 minutes before class start time.
  • Booking in advance is required for certain classes. Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours prior.
  • Inform instructors of any injuries or physical conditions before class begins.

5. Personal Training

  • Sessions must be scheduled in advance with our certified trainers.
  • Late arrivals may result in shorter sessions without refund or rescheduling.
  • 24-hour notice is required for cancellations to avoid charges.

6. Health & Safety

  • Members must complete a health declaration upon registration.
  • Do not use equipment if feeling unwell or under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
  • Report any malfunctioning equipment or injuries to staff immediately.

7. Pool & Sauna (If Applicable)

  • Shower before entering the pool or sauna.
  • No diving, running, or rough play in the pool area.
  • Sauna sessions are limited to 20 minutes per person.

8. Kids Fitness Zone

  • Children must be supervised by a guardian or staff at all times.
  • Only registered children are allowed in the Kids Zone during designated hours.
  • Parents must sign in their child and provide emergency contact info.

9. Nutrition Bar & Lounge

  • No outside food or beverages allowed.
  • Maintain cleanliness and dispose of trash properly.

10. Termination of Membership

  • Violation of rules may result in suspension or termination of membership without refund.
  • FitZone reserves the right to refuse service or membership at its discretion.

By enrolling as a member, you agree to abide by all FitZone Fitness Center's rules and regulations. These guidelines are subject to change without prior notice.
